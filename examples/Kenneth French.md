# Kenneth French

## Profile

Roth Family Distinguished Professor of Finance at the Tuck School of Business, Dartmouth College. Co-creator of the Fama-French factor models and maintainer of the Kenneth French Data Library — the most widely used source of factor return data in finance research. The Fama-French collaboration has produced 30+ papers together over four decades.
